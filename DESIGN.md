# Design System: Cardinal AI "The System"
**Project ID:** cardinal-ai-rebuild-v2

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
The atmosphere is "Sovereign Cinematic" – a high-end, authoritative, and deeply focused environment. It avoids typical SaaS "bubbles and blobs" in favor of sharp geometry, intense focus points, and large-scale, high-contrast typography. The "vibe" is one of total control and refined technical intelligence.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
*   **Deep Void Black (#050505)**: The canvas for the entire experience. Provides depth and removes distractions.
*   **Cardinal Pulse (#C41E3A)**: A vivid, richly saturated crimson. Used sparingly for high-value highlights, active data pipelines, and primary CTA.
*   **Glacier White (#FFFFFF)**: Pure white for primary headings to ensure maximum impact and readability.
*   **Subdued Mercury (rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.4))**: Muted text for secondary information, creating a sophisticated visual hierarchy.

## 3. Typography Rules
*   **Heading 1 (Outfit)**: Ultra-bold (800), letter-spacing (-0.05em). Designed to dominate the screen.
*   **Body (Inter)**: Medium weight (400), clean sans-serif. Used for concise, punchy narrative text.

## 4. Component Stylings
*   **Primary CTA Button**: Sharp, squared-off edges. Cardinal Pulse background with crisp white text. Transforms on hover with a subtle scale and glow.
*   **Cinematic Canvas**: Full-bleed, edge-to-edge. "Cover" behavior. Features a subtle "film grain" overlay to ground the digital visuals.
*   **Interactive Modal**: Full-screen backdrop-blur (20px) using Deep Void Black (#000000cc).

## 5. Layout Principles
*   **Minimalist Density**: Extreme whitespace. The goal is one single focused idea per scroll section.
*   **Visual-First Hierarchy**: The image sequence dictates the layout. Text exists only to anchor the visual story.
*   **Symmetry & Balance**: Centered compositions for maximum authority and premium feel.
